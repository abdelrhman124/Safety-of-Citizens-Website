    Safety of Citiezn

    The site works for the security and safety of the citizen and to protect him from the dangers he faces, whether by human intervention or natural factors, and to protect him from falling into danger, so we work to help him avoid them through the site. The state is in its best condition in terms of security and safety for the citizen, and making the public facilities and roads in the best and most beautiful condition.
